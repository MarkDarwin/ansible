# Naming Conventions

## Goals
Provide consistent naming across languages and tools.

## Rules
- PowerShell: PascalCase functions, camelCase variables.
- Bash: lowercase_with_underscores.
- Ansible: snake_case variables.
- Filenames: lowercase-hyphens.
- Dates should always be in the format: yyyy-mm-dd

## Known Gaps
- Resource naming for cloud environments
